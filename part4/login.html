Privacy Notice

Data collected

When you sign in, we only use the information needed to open your session. Your email address and password are sent securely to our API, and a session token is then stored in your browser so you can stay connected while you browse.

Cookie used

We use a single cookie called token. It keeps your session active, expires after 1 day, and is only there for authentication. It is not used for tracking, analytics, or advertising.

Your rights (GDPR / RGPD)

  • You can ask to correct inaccurate information
  • You can ask to delete your data

If you want to exercise one of these rights, contact the site administrator. You can also end your session at any time by logging out or clearing your browser cookies.
